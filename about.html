THE FACE BEHIND L’AMOUR NAILS

I am Sapana, originally from Nepal- the land of Mount Everest,

I have always had passion in business and it has been a dream come true owning L’amour Nails. Since young age, I have always enjoyed painting and designing my own nails as well as doing it for my friends and family as a hobby.

With roller-coaster rides in life, post Whipple-surgery in 2017, during recovery, I realised doing nails and art work is what I love. Therefore, I decided to master skills and boost my confidence in nail industry from Monaco Nail Academy. I am fully qualified and NZ certified nail artist from 2018, since I turned my passion into profession.

I currently operate a home based boutique nail studio with a desire to provide premium manicure service personalized to your individual needs. To keep up to date with current trends and provide my clients with finest nail care service I regularly up-skill.

I love hand painted nail art, and get creative with pigments, chrome or glitter to follow the latest trend.

I am pet lover and I have a super cute fur baby- Rocco is very friendly, jolly and loves to meet new people. During my free time, I love to travel, decorate and pamper my fur baby.

- Sapana Simkhada -
